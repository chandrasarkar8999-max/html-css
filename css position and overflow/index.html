Technology plays a central role in shaping modern society, deeply affecting the way we live, work, and interact with one another. It has transformed communication by making it faster and more accessible through tools like social media, email, and instant messaging. From smartphones and laptops to advanced software and artificial intelligence, digital tools make tasks faster and more efficient.
